We believe change is never the work of one person — it's the work of minds that choose to unite.

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, operating across India through a network of dedicated professionals and volunteers.

We're registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 80G & 12A certified for donor tax exemptions, CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, NITI Aayog registered, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certified.

Education First

Bridging access gaps through mentorship, digital literacy, and skilling initiatives.

Community Led

Programs are co-designed with the communities they serve, not delivered to them.

Transparent Impact

Every donation and volunteer hour is tracked against a stated outcome.

Youth Powered

Run largely by student volunteers and early-career interns across the country.

What we run

Six projects, one shared mission

Each project has a clear focus area and a measurable outcome behind it.

Se

Project SEVA

Food and clothing distribution to the underprivileged — over 50,000+ meals and garments delivered so far.

Ba

Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children through digital literacy, life skills, and school support.

Je

Project JEEV

Animal welfare on the ground — feeding 50+ stray animals daily through our volunteer network.

Ud

Project UDAAN

Women's empowerment with rural self-help groups — financial independence, skilling, and hygiene awareness.

Pr

Project PRAKRITI

Environmental conservation through 20,000+ saplings planted and support for eco-friendly agriculture.

Vi

Project VIKAS

Employability and internships across data, finance, research, content, and marketing — 30,000+ interns trained in four years.
